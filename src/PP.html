Information Collected By Using ID Al. Solutions Website

  • “Cookies” are data files that may be stored on your computer (or other devices, such as a smartphone or tablet) when you visit the ID Al. Solutions website. The website sends information to the browser which then creates a text file.
  • Cookies allow us to recognize you when you return. They also help us provide a customized experience and can enable us to detect certain kinds of fraud. A cookie will usually contain the name of the website the cookie has come from, time, and value.
  • Most browsers allow you to control cookies, including whether to accept them or not and to remove them. You may set most browsers to notify you if you receive a cookie, or you may choose to block cookies with your browser.
  • Pixel Tags, Web Beacons, and Trackers
  • Pixel tags, web beacons, and tracking URLs are tiny graphic images and/or small blocks of code placed on website pages, ads, or emails that allow us to determine whether you performed a specific action. When you access these pages, or when you open an email, let us know you have accessed the web page or opened the email. These tools help us measure the response to our communications and improve our web pages and promotions.
